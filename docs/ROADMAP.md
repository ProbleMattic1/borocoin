
# Roadmap (90-Day)

**Phase 1: MVP**
- Local ledger, roles, EARN/REDEEM/ISSUE
- Daily anchors; basic reports

**Phase 2: Pilot**
- Proper auth (OTP), QR codes + scanner
- Settlement exports; anomaly alerts

**Phase 3: Hardening**
- Rate limiting; expiry policy
- POS webhooks (optional)
- PoA network + contract
